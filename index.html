TRULY ANONYMOUS

Let's Connect is truly anonymous! Privacy is very important to us, so employees' identity is never shared with the companies. This confidence encourages employees to be honest and fearless about their feedback.

FEEDBACK SHOULD BE PRIVATE

Employee feedback should be private, only visible to the leadership of your organization. Unlike other anonymous feedback services which allow employees to share anonymous feedback publicly, the feedback shared on let's connect is only visible to the company leadership.

INITIATED BY EMPLOYEES

Let employees share what is bothering them, how they can add value and improve your business. Let them a hero in the growth story of your organization.

A COMMUNICATION CHANNEL

The problem with typical anonymous feedback is that communicating back to the aggrieved employee is difficult. With Let's connect it's a breeze to talk to employees without them revealing their identity. A communication channel which makes sure that employees are kept in loop.
