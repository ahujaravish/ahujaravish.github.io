FEEDBACK SHOULD BE TRULY ANONYMOUS

Let's Connect is truly anonymous! Privacy is very important to us, so employees' identity is never shared with the leadership. This confidence encourages employees to be honest and fearless about their feedback.

FEEDBACK SHOULD BE PRIVATE

Only your employees can give feedback, we use innovative and easy to use technology to make sure no one from outside your company can provide feedback. Moreover feedback is only visible to the leadership of your organization. Unlike other anonymous feedback services which allow employees to share anonymous feedback publicly, the feedback shared on let's connect is only visible to the company leadership.

FEEDBACK SHOULD BE DIRECT

Often times the feedback given by employees do not reach the leadership which can take quick measures to fix the problems. Let's connect provides a direct link between employees and the leadership.

FEEDBACK SHOULD BE REAL-TIME

The time for annual employee engagement surveys is coming to an end, conducting annual surveys means the results are more likely to be skewed by unusual events (e.g., layoffs, mergers, product initiatives). It's time for Real-time approach to measure and drive high levels of employee commitment and passion.

INITIATED BY EMPLOYEES

Let employees share what is bothering them, how they can add value and improve your business. Let them be a hero in the growth story of your organization.

TWO WAY COMMUNICATION CHANNEL

The problem with typical anonymous feedback is that it is one way, communicating back to the employees is difficult. With Let's connect it's a breeze talking to employees without them revealing their identity. Two way communication channel which makes sure that employees are kept in loop.

How does it work?

As easy as taking a picture

  1. Employee opens the LetsConnect app on her smartphone
  2. Points the camera towards a QR Code (provided to organization at signup or on-demand, company can post QR Code in common areas in the office or on company intranet)
  3. Write the feedback and hit the Submit button

Easy, right?

  • Remember, it's a two way street, so the organization representative can reply back to the feedback seamlessly, without the employee having to reveal her identity
